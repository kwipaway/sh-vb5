Frequently Asked Questions

General Benefits

Students must apply through the VA website at va.gov. After approval, submit your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) through the SHSU certification portal.

The COE is issued by the VA and confirms your eligibility for education benefits. This document is required before SHSU can process your benefits.

Certification Process

All requests must be submitted through the online certification portal. Documents sent by email, mail, or fax will not be accepted.

Processing typically takes 2–4 weeks after all required documents are received. During peak periods, processing times may be longer.

Yes. Students must submit a certification request each semester they plan to use benefits.

Hazlewood Act

The Hazlewood Act provides qualified Texas veterans, spouses, and dependents with up to 150 credit hours of tuition exemption at public Texas institutions.

All Hazlewood applications must be submitted before the fee payment deadline each semester.

Payments & Enrollment

This depends on your benefit chapter. Some benefits pay the school directly, while others provide payments to the student.

Students should still secure their classes. Contact the Veterans Benefits Processing Team if you have concerns about payment deadlines.
