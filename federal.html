VETERAN AFFAIRS BENEFITS

VA (Veterans Affairs) educational benefits are federal benefits earned by a service member or veteran. Benefit entitlement can change due to length of service, discharge characterization, as well as home of record/place of entry. For more information or to see if you qualify visit the VA website.
