VETERANS BENEFITS PROCESSING

Use Your Benefits

Created in 2023, our department provides personalized assistance as you navigate utilizing your veteran and dependent education benefits. Whether you are a veteran, an active service member, or a dependent, our team ensures your transition to SHSU is seamless.

Access Online Certification Portal Notice: All documents must be submitted through our online portal. Emailed, faxed, or mailed documents will not be accepted.

Federal Benefits

VA educational benefits are federal benefits earned by a service member or veteran. Entitlement can change due to length of service, discharge characterization, and home of record.

Stop waiting on hold—use our step-by-step portal to upload your Certificate of Eligibility (COE) and register your classes for the upcoming term.

Hazlewood Benefit

The Lone Star State thanks you for your service. The Hazlewood Act provides eligible veterans, spouses, and dependents with up to 150 credit hours of tuition exemption.

Submit your Hazlewood semester waivers directly through the portal before the fee payment deadline to ensure your account is credited on time.
